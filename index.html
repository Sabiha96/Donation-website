Thalassemia Foundation Zakat Fund

The zakat fund is the lifeline for a large number of our patients. The number of beneficiary patients has increased steadily over the past few years.
However, many patients could not come to the hospital in 2020 due to the corona virus pandemic.

How to donate?

You can donate online using a Visa, Mastercard, or American Express credit card. You can also deposit or e-transfer to our bank accounts, or use bKash to make payment from your mobile banking account.
